Welcome

PartyAll is an web app that allows users to collectively curate a playlist of songs. PartyAll will automatically play songs that have been requested and voted by users.

What type of user are you?

Host
Choose Host if you're the host of your party. Music will play from your device and you have special control over the song queue.
Guest
Choose Guest if you're at a party hosted by someone else. Make sure you have your party key ready!
